Narwhals

Narwhals are known as the "unicorns of the sea," but unlike unicorns, narwhals are real animals living in the Arctic Ocean!

  • Scientific Name: Monodon monoceros
  • Average Length: 4.5 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 30-40 years
  • Habitat: Arctic Ocean

Narwhals are whales that live in the Arctic Ocean. Their most prominent feature is a large spiral tusk.
